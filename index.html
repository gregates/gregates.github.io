About Greg Gates (that's me)

I used to be a philosopher. I guess that sticks with you, so maybe I still am.

These days I'm also a software developer, working primarily in Ruby and Javascript but very eager to learn more.

You can read a bit about my experience in making the jump from academia to coding on WebKite's blog (that's my current employer, and the company that took a chance on training me from scratch).

Please don't hesitate to get in touch.

I'm a novice at remote pair programming but would love to try it. I'd say at this point I'm pretty knowledgeable about Ruby and JavaScript, including several framework flavors of each (Rails, Sinatra, Ember, Backbone, etc.). I'd love to have a real opportunity to learn something new, such as Rust or Haskell or really any compiled language.

I really enjoy working with people who are new to web development. But I am still learning myself, and could also benefit a lot from working closely with more experienced developers.
